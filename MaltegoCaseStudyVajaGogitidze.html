CRM Data Quality & Gamification

Feedback conversation framework and adoption strategy

‚úì Key Assumptions

  • Root cause: Poor CRM hygiene stems from friction (too complex/time-consuming), not malicious intent or laziness.
  • Sales motivation: Account Managers care most about commission speed and reducing admin interruptions, not forecast accuracy.
  • Technical capability: HubSpot/Salesforce can automatically calculate "Opportunity Health Score" via workflow automation.
  • Tier benefits: Fast quote turnaround is more valuable to AMs than gift cards or recognition alone.
  • Team dynamics: Peer accountability (team goal) is effective without creating toxic competition if framed correctly.
  • RevOps bandwidth: Solution must be automated; manual scoring or enforcement won't scale.

1. Preparation (Evidence-Based)

  • Pull 2-3 specific examples where missing CRM data caused delays
  • Example: "Your Q4 deal sat in legal for 4 days waiting for billing entity‚Äîdelayed commission"
  • Identify pattern: Which fields are consistently missing?
  • Come with facts, not opinions

2. The Conversation

Opening (Direct & Collaborative)

"I want to make quotes faster for you. Right now, missing CRM data is slowing us both down‚Äîlet's fix it together."

Frame Impact Around THEIR Pain:

  • Missing billing contact ‚Üí Finance can't draft contract ‚Üí deal stuck in legal ‚Üí commission delayed
  • Incomplete deal data ‚Üí I ping you 3 times for info ‚Üí interrupts your selling time
  • Missing close dates ‚Üí Can't prioritize your quote requests ‚Üí slower turnaround

Solution: Reduce Required Fields

Only 4 fields are mandatory before deal close:

Required Field Why It Matters
Close Date Quote prioritization
Next Step Deal progression tracking
Decision Maker Contact Contract routing
Billing Contact Invoice processing

Everything else = optional.

This approach creates a win-win:

Clean CRM becomes a by-product of speed, not an administrative burden.
